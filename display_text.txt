## Project Overview

The **Goldfish Project** aims to provide a better way to accumulate protein data for closed-loop protein design. It was authored by Max Witwer, Jie Chen, and Elliot Cole. This initiative is a collaborative effort with the Baker Lab and was developed during an internship at the Institute for Protein Design.
