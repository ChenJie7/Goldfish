  <h2>Project Overview</h2>
  <p>
    The <strong>Goldfish Project</strong> aims to provide a better way to accumulate protein data for closed-loop protein design. It was authored by Max Witwer, Jie Chen, and Elliot Cole. This initiative is a collaborative effort with the Baker Lab and was developed during an internship at the Institute for Protein Design.
